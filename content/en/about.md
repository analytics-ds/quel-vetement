## Our mission

Quel Vêtement is an editorial outlet dedicated to menswear. Our goal is simple: help every man make the right clothing choice based on his build, budget and the occasion.

Menswear can quickly become a headache. Cuts, fabrics, sizes that vary from one brand to another, trends that change every season. We sort it all out with clear benchmarks and concrete advice.

## What you will find here

- Detailed buying guides for every type of clothing
- Comparisons between brands, cuts and fabrics
- Style and care advice
- Reliable, verified sizing references

## The author

Quel Vêtement is written by **Maxime Coste**, a writer specialised in menswear, who tests, compares and filters to keep only what is worth it.

## What to wear for every occasion

Knowing **what to wear** shouldn't be a headache. Between unspoken dress codes, company guidelines and last-minute surprises, we often end up hesitating in front of the wardrobe. The idea behind Quel Vêtement is simple: start from your real situation (an occasion, a place, the weather, a level of dress) and guide you, piece by piece, towards the most fitting outfit.

We don't just tell you what to put on, we explain why: which suit cut for a wedding, which shirt for a job interview, which shoes to wear with chinos. Clear, concrete benchmarks so you never have to wonder what to wear again.

## Choosing your clothes by situation, season and style

There is no "right outfit" in a vacuum: it depends on the context. A single day can call for office wear in the morning and an evening look at night. Our guides cover the key situations of a man's life and every family of garments, from suits to linen, from shirts to sneakers, to help you build a coherent wardrobe and know, at a glance, what to wear and when.

> **In short:** A men's shirt comes down to three details: the cut, the collar and the fabric. Combined well, they change everything.

## The collar, first marker

The spread collar opens the face and works with a tie. The button-down feels casual and is worn open.

- French collar: versatile
- Spread collar: dressy
- Button-down: casual

## The cut that falls right

A well-cut shirt does not pull between the buttons and leaves two fingers at the collar. Neither a sack nor a second skin.

> **In short:** Jacket or coat, the question is quickly settled once you know the season and the level of dress you want.

## The right piece by season

The light jacket covers mid-season. The wool coat takes over in winter, the parka when it rains.

- Blouson: casual, mid-season
- Wool coat: dressy, winter
- Parka: practical and waterproof

## Length matters

A coat ideally stops at mid-thigh. Too short it stacks you up, too long it weighs the silhouette down.

> **In short:** A good basic shows in its fabric, its cut and its seams. The rest is detail.

## T-shirt or polo by occasion

The t-shirt plays it casual, the polo steps up and even works at the office in smart-casual mode.

- Crew neck: the simplest
- V-neck: lengthens the torso
- Polo: smart casual

## Quality to the touch

A dense cotton (180 g/m2 or more) keeps its shape wash after wash. Side seams stop the garment from twisting.

> **In short:** Choosing a suit is first a matter of cut and body shape, before it is a question of colour.

## Cut above all

The cut decides 80% of the result. A well-set shoulder and a jacket that hangs straight beat an expensive fabric that fits poorly.

- Slim: for slender builds
- Regular: the safe, comfortable bet
- Tailored: shapes the waist without squeezing

## The right fabric for the season

Wool stays the reference all year. Linen and cotton take over in summer, flannel in winter.

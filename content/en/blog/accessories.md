> **In short:** It is often accessories that turn a decent outfit into a successful one, without a big budget.

## The essentials

A good belt, a scarf in winter and a sober watch are enough to structure most looks.

- Belt: match it to the shoes
- Scarf: the warmth touch
- Watch: sobriety pays

## Match without overdoing it

Simple rule: match the belt to the shoes and limit bright colours to a single piece.

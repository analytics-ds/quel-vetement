> **In short:** Light, breathable and tough, linen is the summer star for the man who wants to stay cool without losing style.

## Why linen in summer

Linen wicks moisture and dries fast. Creases are part of its charm, no need to chase a perfectly pressed look.

- Linen shirt: with chinos or shorts
- Linen trousers: smart-casual look
- Linen jacket: to soften a strict suit

## Care without mistakes

Wash at 30 C, air dry. Avoid the tumble dryer, which shrinks and breaks the fibres.

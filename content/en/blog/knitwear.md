> **In short:** Between a warm jumper and a more adjustable cardigan, the choice mostly depends on your use and the fabric.

## Jumper or cardigan?

The jumper is warmer and structures an outfit. The cardigan buttons up and adapts to the temperature, ideal in mid-season.

- Crew neck: the all-rounder
- Roll neck: warm and elegant
- Cardigan: adjustable

## Fabric makes the warmth

Merino wool warms without itching. Cashmere is softer and pricier, cotton more breathable for indoors.

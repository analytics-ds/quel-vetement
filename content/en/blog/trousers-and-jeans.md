> **In short:** The right trousers respect your body shape and the use you make of them, from the office to the weekend.

## Choosing by body shape

A straight cut balances most builds. Slim suits slim legs, carrot offers room at the thighs.

- Straight: the most versatile
- Slim: slender build
- Chino: between jeans and dress trousers

## Jeans or dress trousers

Raw jeans dress a casual look. Chinos or wool trousers step in when you need one notch more.
